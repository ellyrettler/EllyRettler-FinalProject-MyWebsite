Hello!

What is this site?

This is a site created for my web design class. The pourpose of this site is to keep a record of some of the books I have read over the years, along with my personal opinions on them. I made this site to provide honest reviews for anyone interested in finding a new book to read, while also providing a database of the books I’ve read and want to read over the years. You can access the website’s content using the navigation bar at the top of the page. Feel free to explore the site, and click on some books for their full reveiws, and enjoy my first website!

Who am I?

Hello, my name is Ellyana Rettler, and I am currently a DVC student at the University of Wisconsin–Milwaukee. I live in a rural area in West Bend, Wisconsin, where I enjoy spending time with my family and friends, reading books, practicing art, and playing video games. As mentioned, this is the first website I have ever built, and I thought it would be fun to base it on some of my hobbies. I hope you enjoy the site and gain some insight into a few of the books I like.
